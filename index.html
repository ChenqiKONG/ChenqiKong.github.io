Short Bio

I am a fourth-year Ph.D. candidate in the Department of Computer Science at City University of Hong Kong, under the supervision of Prof. WANG Shiqi. My research interests mainly focus on multimedia forensics and AI security. Throughout my Ph.D., I have authored several papers published in IEEE TIFS and IEEE TCSVT and co-authored some top-tier journals and conferences, such as IEEE TIP and ICML. Currently, I am on a visiting program at the ROSE Lab, NTU, where I am working on image manipulation detection and localization under the supervision of Prof. Alex C. Kot. I achieved a CGPA of 4.00/4.30 during my Ph.D., and my GPA ranked 3rd among all 143 students in my graduate study. My academic excellence was recognized with numerous scholarships, including National Scholarship and Research Tuition Scholarship.
