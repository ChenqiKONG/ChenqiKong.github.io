Short Bio

I am currently a research fellow at the ROSE Lab, NTU, where I am working on image manipulation detection and localization under the supervision of Prof. Alex C. Kot. I received my B.S. and M.S. degrees from Harbin Institute of Technology in Harbin, China, in 2017 and 2019, respectively. I earned my Ph.D. from the Department of Computer Science at City University of Hong Kong in Hong Kong, China (Hong Kong SAR), in 2023. My research interests encompass AI security and multimedia forensics. I have authored/co-authored several papers published in IEEE TPAMI, IEEE TIFS, IEEE TDSC, IEEE TCSVT, IEEE TIP, ICCV, and ICML. My academic excellence was recognized with numerous scholarships, including National Scholarship, gold medal at the International Exhibition of Inventions in Geneva, and Research Tuition Scholarship.
